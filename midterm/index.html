The Story of Carey Price

Carey Price is known as one of the greatest goalies of all time and arguably the greatest goalie of the 2000's. Born on August 16th, 1987, Price was drafted 5th overall by the Montreal Canadiens in 2005, Price was seen as a controversial pick, as goalies aren't seen to be picked that high. But it certaintly worked out as he became one of the best to ever do it.

More Facts

  • Drafted 5th overall by the Montreal Canadiens
  • One of few goalies to win Rookie of the Year and MVP
  • Played on the same team his entire career, up until this last season
